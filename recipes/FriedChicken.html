Buttermilk Fried Chicken

With this recipe, you can learn to make this delicious fried chicken

Here is a recipe for the best buttermilk fried chicken you've ever had! Easy to make, with just a few ingredients,

you and your family will enjoy all the goodness that this juicy, crispy treat has to offer for dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 pound) frying chicken, cut up
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon celery seeds
  • Vegetable oil for deep-frying
  • 2 cups Kikkoman Tempura batter mix

Steps

  1. Wash the cut up chicken and pat dry
  2. Stir together the buttermilk, garlic powder, onion powder, poultry seasoning and celery seeds
  3. Put the chicken in the mixture
  4. place in a refridgerator for at least 8 hours
  5. In a large frying pan, heat oil to 325 degrees F
  6. Drain chicken in a colander, removing excess mixture.
  7. Place tempura batter mix in large paper bag, add chicken
  8. Close top and shake gently to coat chicken entirely
  9. Remove chicken and fry, turning pieces over every 3 minutes. Continue frying until browned on all sides
